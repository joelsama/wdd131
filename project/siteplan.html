Brew Roots

Site Name

Name: Brew Roots – Local Coffee Roasters

This name reflects the brand’s local, earthy, and authentic roots in craft coffee roasting. It evokes natural ingredients, artisan methods, and a grounded connection to community.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an informational and promotional platform for a fictional small-batch coffee roasting company. Visitors can learn about Brew Roots, explore coffee roasts, and get in touch for general questions or wholesale inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What roast is best for someone who enjoys a smooth, balanced coffee?
  • How can I contact Brew Roots to ask about wholesale orders?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #6f4e37 (used in headers, nav background, and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #fdfbf9 (used for backgrounds, clean/light layout)

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif – clean, modern, and professional.
  • Body Font: Also 'Segoe UI' for consistency and readability.
